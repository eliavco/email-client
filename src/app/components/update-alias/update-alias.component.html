Aliases

are like nicknames which come before the email address (@mail.eliavco.com in our case) so we know we need to direct them to your inbox. For example, if you took the alias 'hello', now all emails sent to 'hello@mail.eliavco.com' will be redirected to your inbox

You can have as many aliases as you want, as long as they do not belong to anybody already
